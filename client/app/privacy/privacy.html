Privacy Policy

What do we do with your information?

When registering on or using certain features on our site, as appropriate, you may be asked to enter your name, email address or other details. We may also collect some information you enter on forms, either online or at events. We use the information we collect from you in the following ways:

  • To send periodic emails notifying you of account information, upcoming events, and other information that may interest you
  • To personalize your experience on our website

How do we protect your information?

To protect your personal information, we take reasonable precautions and follow industry best practices to make sure it is not inappropriately lost, misused, accessed, disclosed, altered or destroyed.

Your personal information is contained behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights to such systems, and are required to keep the information confidential.

Do we use 'cookies'?

Yes. We use cookies to manage your session while you are logged onto our site. The cookie contains a unique token which the site uses to identify you as a logged in user. You may disable cookies if you wish, but you will be unable to use any features that require you to log in.

Third-party disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personal information, unless required to do so by law.

Third-party links

When you click on links on our website, they may direct you away from our site. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites and encourage you to read their privacy statements.

Contact Us

If there are any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us using the contact form on our site, or send an email to privacy@m.cubingza.org
